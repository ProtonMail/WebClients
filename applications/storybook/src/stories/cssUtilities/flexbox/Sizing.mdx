# Item Sizing

Use `.flex-1` or `.flex-auto` on flex items to set the sizing behaviour.

**Resize containers to see the effect.**

`.flex-1` will take as little space as it's content allows (will wrap text).

<div className="border p-2 rounded bg-weak" style={{ width: '320px', resize: 'both', overflow: 'auto' }}>
    <Story id="css-utilities-flexbox-sizing--flex-1" />
</div>

`.flex-auto` will take as little space as it's content allows (will not wrap text).

<div className="border p-2 rounded bg-weak" style={{ width: '320px', resize: 'both', overflow: 'auto' }}>
    <Story id="css-utilities-flexbox-sizing--flex-auto" />
</div>

`.grow-2` will grow by factor 2.

<div className="border p-2 rounded bg-weak" style={{ width: '320px', resize: 'both', overflow: 'auto' }}>
    <Story id="css-utilities-flexbox-sizing--grow" />
</div>

`.shrink-0` This item will not shrink and not wrap text.

<div className="border p-2 rounded bg-weak" style={{ width: '320px', resize: 'both', overflow: 'auto' }}>
    <Story id="css-utilities-flexbox-sizing--shrink-0" />
</div>

`.grow-0` This item will shrink but it will not grow.

<div className="border p-2 rounded bg-weak" style={{ width: '320px', resize: 'both', overflow: 'auto' }}>
    <Story id="css-utilities-flexbox-sizing--grow-0" />
</div>

`.grow-0 .shrink-0` This item will not shrink and not grow.

<div className="border p-2 rounded bg-weak" style={{ width: '320px', resize: 'both', overflow: 'auto' }}>
    <Story id="css-utilities-flexbox-sizing--grow-0-shrink-0" />
</div>

`.flex-none` This item will not flex.
