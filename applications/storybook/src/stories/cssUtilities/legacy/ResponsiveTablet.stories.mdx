<div class="flex flex-justify-space-between mb-8 on-mobile-flex-column">
    <div class="w49 flex-align-self-center">

    </div>

            format

## Margin/padding helpers

Please check before how <a href="?path=/docs/front-end-helpers-margin-padding--page">margin/padding helpers</a> work.

-   <code>.on-tablet-m</code> = <code>margin</code>
-   <code>.on-tablet-p</code> = <code>padding</code>
-   <code>t/r/b/l</code> = <code>top/right/bottom/left</code>
-   <code>0/1/2</code> = 0/1em/2em

Examples:

<div class="flex flex-justify-space-between mb-4 on-mobile-flex-column">
    <div class="w49 mb-4 md:mb-0 border">
        <div class="on-tablet-pt2 bg-primary ">
            <code>.on-tablet-pt2</code> = <code>padding-top: 2em</code>{' '}
            <span class="color-white">on tablet and smaller</span>.
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="w49 mb-4 md:mb-0 border">
        <div class="on-tablet-pl2 bg-primary">
            <code>.on-tablet-pl2</code> = <code>padding-left: 2em</code>{' '}
            <span class="color-white">on tablet and smaller</span>.
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
<div class="flex flex-justify-space-between mb-8 on-mobile-flex-column">
    <div class="w49 mb-4 md:mb-0 border">
        <div class="on-tablet-p1 bg-primary">
            <code>.on-tablet-p1</code> = <code>padding: 1em</code>{' '}
            <span class="color-white">on tablet and smaller</span>.
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="w49 mb-4 md:mb-0 border">
        <div class="on-tablet-pl1 on-tablet-pr1 m-4 lg:m-0 bg-primary">
            <code>.on-tablet-pl1</code> + <code>.on-tablet-pr1</code> + <code>.m-4 .lg:m-0</code> ={' '}
            <code>padding-left: 1em</code> + <code>padding-right: 1em</code> + <code>margin: 1em</code>{' '}
            <span class="color-white">on tablet and smaller</span>.
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

## Width/max-width helpers

<div>
    <ul class="list-2columns on-tablet-list-1column">
