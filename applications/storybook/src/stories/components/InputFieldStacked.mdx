# InputFieldStacked

`InputFieldStacked` is a wrapper of `inputField` component. It's meant to mimic Pass-like inputs.

<Canvas withSource="open">
    <Story id="components-inputfieldstacked--basic" />
</Canvas>

`icon` attribute is not mandatory, you can apply the `bigger` prop, and you can make the field bold.

<Canvas withSource="open">
    <Story id="components-inputfieldstacked--bold-example" />
</Canvas>

## Group

In case of grouped fields, you may use `InputFieldStackedGroup` component. Don't forget to add `isGroupElement` attribute to `InputFieldStacked` component ;)

Other types of fields are supported: `select`, `textarea`, etc.
