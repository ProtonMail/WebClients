Welcome to ProtonMail 4.0 Introducing ProtonCalendar Have questions or feedback?

ProtonMail 4.0 is a brand new inbox which contains many new features while continuing to improve the privacy and security that Proton is known for.

Here’s an overview of some of the new features.

Meet the new app menu

All Proton services (Mail, Calendar, Contacts) are available from the Sidebar

ProtonCalendar is a new encrypted calendar to go with ProtonMail. Now you can create events which are encrypted in a way that even we cannot see the details of your events.

ProtonCalendar beta can be accessed by all paid Proton users (it will also be made available to free users later on.)
