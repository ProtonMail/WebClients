ProtonMail uses a two password system to secure your account.

Login Password

The first password is your Login Password. This password is sent to our server so we can make sure you are actually the person signing into your account.

We use this to authenticate you and log you into our system.

Mailbox Password

The second password is your Mailbox Password. This password is used to decrypt your messages in your browser. This password never goes to our server so even we can't read your messages.

We never see this second password, we cannot recover it for you.

Password Managers such as LastPass may cause login issues and are not recommended for use during our Beta. Learn More
